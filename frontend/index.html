Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

Stumped on something? Don't see the info you need? No worries! Drop us a line at our support email, and we'll leap into action to help you out ASAP.

What is JobSound.ai?
JobSound.ai is a cutting-edge platform that transforms the hiring process with AI-powered voice interviews. Simply share a URL with candidates, and they can dive into a dynamic interview experience. Our AI will chat with them, capture their responses, and even call directly to their phones if needed.
How does the AI voice interview work?
After an employer shares an interview link, applicants will engage in a conversation with our AI, which asks predetermined questions. Their answers are recorded, and once the interview is complete, our system analyzes the responses based on various metrics to generate a comprehensive analysis report and a potential score for each applicant.
What kind of analysis does JobSound.ai provide?
Our AI analyzes responses for clarity, relevance, confidence, and other soft skills, providing a score and insights into the applicant's strengths and weaknesses. This gives employers a deeper understanding of each candidate beyond their resume.
Can JobSound.ai call applicants on their phone?
Yes, indeed! We offer VOIP service that allow employers to start AI interviews via phone calls, adding another layer of convenience and personal touch to the remote interview experience.
Is the voice of the AI realistic?
We pride ourselves on the realism of our AI voices. They're designed to be engaging and lifelike, making sure that candidates feel as though they're talking to a real person.
How secure is the data collected by JobSound.ai?
We take data security seriously. All interviews and analyses are encrypted and stored securely, with access strictly controlled according to privacy laws and regulations.
